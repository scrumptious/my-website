color picker 
--------------

Color picker is a useful tool that will help you to find a perfect colors set. Whether you're a designer, developer or just making a birthday card for your beloved. This user friendly app will show you a preview of both background and paragraph color and help you with hex code (that strange looking code starting with '#').
R, G, B values means respectively Red, Green and Blue color components.
